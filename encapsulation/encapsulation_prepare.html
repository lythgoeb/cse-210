CSE 210 | Programming with Classes

Encapsulation: Prepare

Purpose

The purpose of this activity is to develop and demonstrate your mastery of the following competencies.

  • Articulate the principles of programming with classes.
    • Explain the principle of encapsulation and why it is important.

Develop Your Competency

Study the Materials

Read or watch the following materials carefully. Keep track of what you are learning in a notebook.

  • Encapsulation: The Second Principle
Ask yourself the following questions. If you can't answer all of them, review the study materials and your notes until you can.
  • What is the definition of encapsulation?
  • What are the benefits of encapsulating classes?
  • What is an access modifier?
  • What is the syntax for applying access modifiers?
  • What is a getter?
  • What is a setter?
  • What is the syntax for specifying a getter?
  • What is the syntax for specifying a setter?

Practice What You've Learned

Review the following example carefully. When you have understood all the content, try to write the code for the missing part of the incomplete project. Compare your work to the completed version when you are finished.

  • game specification
  • game walk-through
  • game source (incomplete)
  • game source (complete)
Feel free to use the completed source code to help you overcome any problems that arise. We all need help and inspiration from time to time. Don't make this decision too quickly though. It's in your best interest to write as much code as you can on your own.

Verify Your Understanding

Explain what you have learned to someone who is not a current student in the course. If you are unsure about any part of your explanation, review the study materials, your notes, or the example project and try again.

Demonstrate Your Mastery

Write a short answer response to the following question. Submit your work through I-Learn when you are finished. Remember, you can try as many times as you need, anytime throughout the course, in order to demonstrate your mastery of the competencies.

What is encapsulation and why is it important?

Remember to illustrate your answer using code from the example program.
