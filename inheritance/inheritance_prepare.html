CSE 210 | Programming with Classes

Inheritance: Prepare

Purpose

The purpose of this activity is to develop and demonstrate your mastery of the following competencies.

  • Articulate the principles of programming with classes.
    • Explain the principle of inheritance and why it is important.

Develop Your Competency

Study the Materials

Read or watch the following materials carefully. Keep track of what you are learning in a notebook.

  • Inheritance: The Third Principle
Ask yourself the following questions. If you can't answer all of them, review the study materials and your notes until you can.
  • What is the definition of inheritance?
  • What are the benefits of inheriting from a class?
  • What is a superclass?
  • What is a subclass?
  • How do you invoke a superclass constructor?
  • How do you invoke a superclass behavior?
  • What is the danger of overusing inheritance?

Practice What You've Learned

Review the following example carefully. When you have understood all the content, try to write the code for the missing part of the incomplete project.

Verify Your Understanding

Demonstrate Your Mastery
